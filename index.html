A bit of history.

The city of Athens has hosted many professional jazz musicans over the years and the people who live here aren’t short of enthusiasts themselves. The first official Athens Jazz Fest of 1998 featured small jazz ensembles from our very own Ohio University and other local communities. Our list of performances have only grown since, and include more well-known guests like the Columbus Youth Orchestra! We also offer jazz clinics and presentations by Ohio University, ARTS/West, Athens Uncorked, Casa Nueva, and Tony’s. Our mission is simple: Get the music out there for everybody and anybody to share and enjoy!

Matt James

Professor of Saxophone and Jazz Studies
